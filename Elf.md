- Elves are defined by an all consuming obsession. They are all the classic elf traits - beautiful, skilled, ethereal etc, but at a cost. They must choose and obsess over a specific craft - be it an art, sport, skill - and if they do not then they begin to die. "The Passage" is the name of this phenomenon - elves who lose interest in their craft begin to physically age, which they would not do otherwise. At the extremes of this process, elves lose the ability to move, their skin roughens like bark, and they eventually become trees.
