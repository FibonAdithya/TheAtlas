- Deep in the jungles the fledgling nation of Manol has been declared neutral territory by the [[Wyrdmarked guilds]] to protect their interests in the [[Elusium]] veins of the land. Acting as the wild west many come to Manol running from their past or for the chance at a great future.

- Truths of Manol:
	- <b> Made for Extraction</b>
		- #+BEGIN_QUOTE
		  "We don't have many rules. Just follow the charter. Don't fuck with the guilds."
		  #+END_QUOTE
		- Manol is not a land of kings, but a land of items. It is governed by the [[Manolan Extraction Charter]] a document writed by the Guilds and signed by the nations. Prospectors and Claim-Stakes are not lawmen; they are hired to ensure that the flow of Elusium is never interrupted by rivals, rebels, or the encroaching green.
	- <b> Great Dinosaurs roam the land</b>
		- #+BEGIN_QUOTE
		  "Beasts like no other"
		  #+END_QUOTE
		- The dinosaurs of Manol are relics of a pre-Imperial age. To the people of Manol, a Great Dinosaur is not merely an animal, but a manifestation of the [[Sovereign Endless]] in scale and tooth. These beasts gorge on raw Elusium veins, bloating their bodies and granting them strange abilities - shimmering hides, breath of frost, or steps that crack the earth. Hunting them is a necessity for survival and a gruesome industry; their [[Beast-marrow]] is the only substance capable of stabilising the most volatile Elusium reactions.
	- <b> A Land of the Mad and Desperate </b>
		- #+BEGIN_QUOTE
		  "Many can light lamps. In Manol, they try to burn the sky.”
		  #+END_QUOTE
		- In the deep, humid dark of the jungle, the [[Treaty of the Crown]] is a distant memory. Because Manol sits upon the world's rawest Elusium deposits, it has become a sanctuary for those who find magic insufficient. Radical mages and Guild-exiles venture into the jungle to experiment with magic that reshapes nations. In Manol, the cost of high magic is often paid in the blood of the desperate, fuelled by the hope that one raw strike of ore can make a man a god.
