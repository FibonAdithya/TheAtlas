- Temperglass is a stormphrax-inspired material found in the Candle Coast. Though [[The Breaking-Kirk]] did employ a shit-ton of it, it traces its origins back to before the first age, in a very limited and solely luxury capacity. It's fair to say that the technology was adapted used practically for the first time ever in the Kirk.
- Its difference from normal glass is in its response to light. If the side of temperglass that you look at is in relative darkness, it is transparent and brittle as normal glass. However, if light shines on the glass, it turns obsidian-black instantly and hardens to become incredibly strong. Not even the full strength of a Planetar suit can break temperglass in light, hence their use of temperhammers as a primary weapon.
