- Dwarves are the remnants of tribes who did not kneel to the emperor on Descension Day. Such folk were driven deep underground, along with dark powers who influenced or directly subjugated them and which still have a hold on them to this day. Dwarves were instrumental in the construction of the god-felling spell, and made a large part of the communities that lived in the sealed temples where the spell was made.
- Apart from House Hohenstav and other isolated communities, most dwarves are still subterranean. Now that almost half a millenium has passed since the Empire fell, more and more of them are surfacing. But the deeper they were, the more likely it turns out to be that they serve the will of some unknowable thing from below.
