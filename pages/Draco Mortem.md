- The name Draco Mortem means dragon killer in an old dialect of the local language.
- Physically, they are two towers in districts of Kingarrock. One in the north, one in the west. Shaped like gigantic gargoyles curled about spurs of rock, they stand over two hundred metres tall (tbc). In times when Kingarrock would need to defend itself from aerial threats such as dragons, these gargoyles would animate. Within their mouths are cannons which may be loaded with all manner of blessed magical ammunitions that the operators may have devised. It is said that all it takes is two shots from a Draco Mortem tower to down a fully grown dragon - and that's why there are two towers.
