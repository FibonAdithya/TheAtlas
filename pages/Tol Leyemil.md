- Main Goal:
	- Protect the realm as a hub of arcane scholarship and magic.
	- Counter the territorial expansions of [[Yor]] to the north, which drew them into the conflict.
- Governance:
	- Ruled by the Arcanix Assembly, a council of mages representing various colleges across the land.
- Military and Intelligence:
	- Military power centered on the [[Knights Arcane]], elite magical warriors.
	- Supported by the Eyes of Tol, a specialized spy network.
	- Ore disruptions during the war hampered the production of essential equipment and weapons for the Knights Arcane.
- Alliances and Relations:
	- Maintained a neutral but slightly allied stance with [[Oystnovi]].
	- Many Cyphermagi receive early education in Tor Leyemil’s colleges, strengthening ties.
	- View [[Vor'ak]] as a backward society due to its fear and persecution of magic, aspiring to conquer and reform the nation.
- Truths -
	- **The Debt of Magic**
		- #+BEGIN_QUOTE
		  "We have the finest brushes and the most perfected techniques in the world, yet we must beg the merchants of Metrol for the ink."
		  #+END_QUOTE
		- Despite their academic mastery, Tol Leyemil finds itself in the shackles of [[Metrol]] who control the supply of [[Elusium]]. Knights Arcane are often forced to ration their components, and the Cinnabar Hearth is searching for a way to synthetically make Elusium.
	- **Through Rune and Rote**
		- #+BEGIN_QUOTE
		  "The common man prays for a miracle; the Scholar calculates the friction of the soul. We do not ‘cast’ magic - we solve it."
		  #+END_QUOTE
		- In Tol Leyemil, magic is stripped of its mystery to ensure it can be taught at scale. This "Science of the First Veil" allows the Arcanix Assembly to field thousands of mages, but it builds a ceiling that few can ever break. Advancement is tied to the Imperial Examinations. A mage who can perfectly recite a thousand "Standard Rotes" gains rank, but this rigid training atrophies the "spiritual muscle" required to pierce the Second Veil.
	- **The Eyes of the Nation**
		- #+BEGIN_QUOTE
		  "The Eyes do not see through vision, but through witness."
		  #+END_QUOTE
		- By sacrificing their own sight to the Endless Yada, the Eyes of Tol become living nodes in a national surveillance web. This constant, invasive presence has created a society of extreme outward performance. In Tol Leyemil, your private thoughts are your only sanctuary, and even those are under threat if you draw the attention of the Arcanix Assembly.
	- **Five Fingers form the Fist**
		- #+BEGIN_QUOTE
		  "Truth is not found; it is won in the crucible of the argument."
		  #+END_QUOTE
		- Each district is governed by a High Chancellor who sits on the Arcanix Assembly. These act as competing schools of thought that define a citizen's entire life path.
			- The Azure Heaven (The Scholasticate of Chronos & Law)
				- **The Function:** This is the administrative heart. They manage the **Treaty of the Crown** and ensure the "Rote" is updated across the nation. They believe that if the math of the universe is balanced, the nation cannot fall.
			- The Cinnabar Hearth (The Scholasticate of Alchemical Industry)
				- **The Function:** They turn the raw materials bought from [[M'angas]] or [[Metrol]] into the "Archaic Batteries" that power the nation. They are the most "working-class" of the mages and the most desperate to find a synthetic alternative to Guild-controlled Elusium.
			- The Jade Root (The Scholasticate of Vitalism & Reform)
				- **The Function:** They handle the health of the populace and the "re-education" of those who fail the exams.
			- The Obsidian Gate (The Scholasticate of Planar Geometry)
				- Primary faction behind the Knights
			- The Silver Quill (The Scholasticate of Thought & Sight)
				- This is the home of the Eyes of Tor. They manage the nation’s intelligence and ensure that the "Common Mind" of Tol Leyemil remains unified. They worship [[Yada]].
